    Kendall Jenner is continuing her streak of perfect looks for a night out.

    Appearing at the grand opening of a FWRD pop-up event in West Hollywood yesterday, the model and FRWD creative director turned heads in a luminescent ivory minidress. The bodycon piece featured a draped one-shoulder detail, a cutout on the back, and a shiny sheen that almost made it look like it was glittering. The 818 founder completed her look with transparent vinyl heels, a white half-moon shoulder bag, and a pair of small gold huggie earrings.

    Beauty-wise, Jenner pulled back her dark brown hair into a tight, high ponytail with her signature middle part. She additionally wore light brown eye shadow and a glossy pale pink lip.

    Emma McIntyre//Getty Images

    Emma McIntyre//Getty Images

    Over the past few weeks, Jenner has been spotted in a number of fashionable party looks.

    For example, Jenner sported a fabulous outfit at each of two parties she recently hosted for 818. At the first, she wore a chocolate-brown leather midi dress with a sharp scoop neckline and swishy white fabric layered underneath the hemline. She paired the dress with pointed-toe black pumps and a matching brown clutch. At the second event, Jenner wore an olive leather set, comprised of a strapless tube top and high-waisted trousers, plus a black leather belt and a black shoulder bag.

    Late last month, Jenner also partied in a little black dress fresh off of David Koma’s fall/winter 2023 runway. The sultry piece featured an illusion tulle neckline and 3-D red floral pasties, the latter of which coordinated with a wispy red scarf she wore wrapped around her neck.
