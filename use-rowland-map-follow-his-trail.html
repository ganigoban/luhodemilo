    Here is how you can use Rowland's map to follow his trail to go a step further in The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest.

    First Published May 5, 2023, 07:30

    In Hogwarts Legacy The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest, Adelaide Oakes will task you to find his uncle Rowland who she thinks is in danger as he hasn't sent an Own since he informed her about his dealings with radical goblins, Ranrok's Loyalists. You will then have to go to Rowland Oakes' campsite location and defeat the goblins. 

    Once done, you will retrieve Rowland Oakes' Map that will include a river, some rocks, and a dungeon marked with a red X, and your task will be to "Use Rowland's Map to follow his trail." This is where many players are getting stuck as they will not get any directions and will have to go to the next location using the map only.

    Thus, to make your job easier, we have explained where you will have to go to complete The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest.

    REMINDER - Hogwarts Legacy was due to launch on PS4 and Xbox One on April 4, but now won't release until 5 May 2023.

    Table of Contents

    How To Use Rowland's Map to Follow His Trail

    In order to "Use Rowland's map to follow his trail," you can pick up your broom and then go towards Korrow Ruins in the southwest direction, following the river stream as indicated in the map.

    Once you reach the Korrow Ruins Floo Flame, go a bit towards the north direction where you will also see a bonfire, and once you reach there, your objective will be completed.

    After that, you can continue your quest by exploring the goblin outpost, finding Rowland's wand, and then finding Rowland by defeating goblins. 

    That is everything you needed to know about using Rowland's map to follow his trail, and upon completing The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest, you will earn a Handcrafted Necklace as a reward. 

    How To Use Rowland's Map To Follow His Trail In Hogwarts Legacy FAQ

    How do I use Rowland's Map to follow his trail in Hogwarts Legacy?

    To use Rowland's Map and follow his trail in Hogwarts Legacy, head southwest towards Korrow Ruins while following the river stream as indicated on the map. Once you reach the Korrow Ruins Floo Flame, head slightly north to a bonfire to complete the objective.

    What reward do I get for completing The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest in Hogwarts Legacy?

    Upon completing The Tale of Rowland Oakes quest in Hogwarts Legacy, you will earn a Handcrafted Necklace as a reward. This serves as a token of your accomplishment in the quest.
