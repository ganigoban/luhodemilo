    Who has Victoria Justice dated?

    Josh Hutcherson

    In early 2008, reports surfaced claiming Victoria Justice was dating Hunger Games star, Josh Hutcherson.

    Throughout the year, the two were spotted arm in arm at various red carpet events.

    In March 2009, Victoria and Josh's rumored relationship made headlines again.

    Around that time, it was reported that the two hit splitsville.

    Ryan Rottman

    From 2011 to 2013, Victoria was in a relationship with another actor, Ryan Rottman.

    After two years together, a source told US Weekly in December 2013 that the pair called it quits.

    The source told the publication: "It happened a while ago. They are at different points in their life and both had been traveling."

    Pierson Fode

    In the wake of the breakup news surrounding Victoria's relationship with Ryan, she moved on to another romance.

    Around the split, rumors swirled of Victoria's coupling with her Naomi and Ely’s No Kiss List co-star, Pierson Fode.

    "They were super affectionate and open about it. Her arms were around him most of the night, dancing and kissing."

    The former Zoey 101 star and her Netflix co-star called it quit in November 2015.

    Reeve Carney

    Where one co-star friendship turned relationship ends, another one begins.

    In 2016, the actress started dating her Rocky Horror Picture Show remake cast member, Reeve Carney.

    In December of that year, the two made one of their first appearances as a couple at a Just Jared holiday party.

    An onlooker from the soiree revealed: "They've been together for a couple of months now and aren't going out of their way to hide it but just don't go out together in public a ton."
