    Here's how to get the new Strand Aspects in Destiny 2 Season of the Deep.

    First Published May 24, 2023, 10:59

    Destiny 2 Season of the Deep has arrived, bringing with it new story missions, replayable activities, weapons  & armor, challenges, and more. Bungie has also added three new Strand Aspects, one for each of the three classes in the game. These are Threaded Spectre (Hunter), Flechette Storm (Titan), and The Wanderer (Warlocks.)

    With this new addition, every class in Destiny 2 has now access to four Strand Aspects. If you plan to play the new season, here's our guide, which explains how to get the new Strand Aspects in Destiny 2 Season of the Deep. 

    How to unlock the new Strand Aspects in Destiny 2 Season of the Deep

    You can get the new Strand Aspects by completing the new quest, "Parting the Veil." However, you won't have access to this quest until you finish the Lightfall campaign and the post-campaign "Unfinished Business" quest. 

    After completing those, head back to Neomuna and talk to Nimbus to start the new "Parting the Veil" quest. There are six steps in this quest, and it could take about an hour to complete them all. 

    After finishing all the objectives, head to Pouka Pond in the Hall of Heroes and meditate to unlock the new Strand Aspects. Thankfully, you won't have to farm Strand Meditations for these. In addition to the new Aspects, completing the "Parting the Veil" quest will also get you the new Epoch Integration Solar Hand Cannon.

    That concludes our guide on how to get the new Strand Aspects in Destiny 2 Season of the Deep.
