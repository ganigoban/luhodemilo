    SIA ACHIEVED mainstream fame after scoring a number of hit singles.

    She became known for her eclectic performances where she managed to hide her face with elaborate costumes and huge wigs.

    Why does Sia cover her face?

    In order to maintain her privacy against the celebrity aspect that comes with her career, Sia Furler usually covers her face.

    Despite her stardom, Sia makes it a point to hide her face at red-carpet events, concerts, performances, and other occasions that are on camera.

    While speaking with James Corden on The Late Late Show, she revealed: "I don't wear this if there aren't cameras around. I only wear this to maintain a modicum of privacy.

    "I was a singer for like 10 or 11 years to mediocre success, and I was an alcoholic and a drug addict.

    Read More on Sia Furler

    "I sobered up and decided I didn't want to be an artist anymore, because I was starting to become a little bit famous, and it was destabilizing in some way."

    The Chandelier singer echoed similar sentiments to Nightline and shared: "I thought, 'What doesn't exist in pop music at the moment?'

    "I don't want to be critiqued about the way that I look on the Internet.

    "I've been writing pop songs for pop stars now for a couple of years and I've become friends with them and see what their life is like and that's not something I want."

    What chronic illness does Sia have?

    On October 4, 2019, Furler revealed she has been suffering from a chronic illness called Eahlers-Danlos.

    Eahler-Danlos affects one out of 5,000 people and results in hypermobile joints, loose, stretchy skin, and ongoing fatigue.

    On Twitter, the singer told fans: "Hey, I’m suffering with chronic pain, a neurological disease, Ehlers danlos and I just wanted to say to those of you suffering from pain, whether physical or emotional, I love you, keep going.

    "Life is f**king hard. Pain is demoralizing, and you're not alone."

    What is Sia's most famous song?

    Out of all the songs Sia has written, her David Guetta collaboration Titanium is said to be her most famous song.

    On December 9, 2011, Guetta released the joint effort as the fifth single from his Nothing But the Beat album.

    Upon release, the single hit number one in the UK and charted within the top 10 in Australia and the US.
