    Wagner began her career as a cultural correspondent for the Center for American Progress. She later moved on to work for Fader Magazine as the editor-in-chief. Her work in journalism began to gain recognition, and she soon found herself working for prominent news outlets such as MSNBC and CBS.

    MSNBC Career

    Wagner’s career took a significant leap when she joined MSNBC in 2010. She hosted the daytime program “Now with Alex Wagner” until 2015. Her insightful political commentary and engaging interviewing style made her a favorite among viewers.

    CBS and Showtime
