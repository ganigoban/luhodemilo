    Jennifer Lopez showed off her effortlessly chic off-duty style yesterday.

    The "Let's Get Loud" singer was spotted during an afternoon outing with her 15-year-old Emme yesterday in Los Angeles yesterday, where the star paired her casual ensemble with some of her signature accessories. The multi-hyphenate wore a slouchy black knitted sweater, which featured flared sleeves and was cropped to reveal her abs, paired with medium-wash jeans and a pair of white, black, and silver Nike sneakers.

    She completed the look with a pair of black aviator sunglasses, large gold hoops, and several rings, while wearing her hair slicked back into a ponytail with subtle makeup and a nude lip. She also carried a small black, crocodile-embossed Hermès Birkin bag.

    thecelebrityfinder/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

    Lopez is often seen with a Birkin in hand, though she usually carries her go-to purse as a gym bag, paired with one of her trusty bling cups. The Marry Me star often transforms the sidewalk between her car and the front door to her dance studio into a runway, including one of both of the accessories with her athleisure looks.

    The style star has had a busy past few months, embarking on a series of fashion marathons while promoting both her Netflix action flick The Mother and the launch of her new cocktail line, Delola. For the film's LA premiere, the star wore a dazzling custom three-piece set by Brunello Cucinelli, comprised of a matching bra top, floor-length skirt, and long coat featuring a subtle ivory pinstripe pattern lined with silver sequins. She continued the shimmering motif with a silver Tyler Ellis clutch and crystal-embellished hoops from Fernando Jorge jewelry.
