    Prince’s estate is the primary source of the Prince family’s wealth. The estate includes his music royalties, unreleased music, and Paisley Park Studios. The estate’s value has been a subject of controversy and legal battles, but it is estimated to be worth over $200 million.

    Music Royalties

    Prince’s music continues to generate significant income through royalties. His extensive catalog of hits is a goldmine that keeps his legacy alive and contributes to the family’s wealth.

    Paisley Park Studios

    Paisley Park Studios, Prince’s former home and recording studio, is now a museum and concert venue. The revenue from tours, merchandise, and events at Paisley Park contributes to the family’s net worth.
