    Amanda Seyfried cut a divinely elegant figure in a sheer gold gown at a Lancôme event in Shanghai on Tuesday.

    The Mean Girls actor looked breathtaking in a sweeping sheer cape dress adorned with gold, pearl, and jewel detailing by Elie Saab. The neckline of her dress was grandly decorated with the embellishments, creating a look worthy of a queen’s throne. Seyfried paired the glorious frock with a pair of heels and kept her jewelry simple with a few gold rings. She rounded off the look with gold eyeshadow, fluttery eyelashes, and a coral tinted lip and wore her hair down in soft waves.

    Dominique Charriau

    The Lancôme brand ambassador stunned again during Paris Fashion Week in September, that time in pink. She celebrated the luxury beauty brand’s makeup and skincare collaboration with the Louvre in a custom Prada gown that featured a crisscross neckline and a thigh-high slit. She paired the museum-worthy look with black stiletto heels, Tabayer jeweled hoop earrings, and gold and silver rings. Makeup artist Genevieve Herr created a playful French-girl beauty look for the occasion, complete with black winged eyeliner and a soft natural lip.

    Despite consistently serving amazingly lavish looks, Seyfried shared with Bazaar that she still feels uncomfortable on the red carpet. She said her stylist Elizabeth Stewart, who she has worked with for years and is behind both aforementioned looks, helps, as she “truly understands her style.”

    “Elizabeth is so savvy—she is so amazing and I trust her so much,” she said in an interview with Bazaar in December. “She has these incredible relationships with designers, and she always finds the pieces that she knows I will like. She understands what will look good on my body and she knows my boundaries with fashion, and I am so grateful for that.”
