    10 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Jennifer Lopez is showing off her glam approach to winter white.

    Yesterday, the singer was photographed in New York City headed to rehearsals for her upcoming headlining performance on Dick Clark's New Year's Rockin' Eve with Ryan Seacrest this week. Though this year's production will be closed off to the public due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic, the show will still be broadcast live from Times Square.

    For her outing in NYC, Lopez wore a chic, monochromatic ensemble that included a white puffer coat, a cream-hued sweat suit, a fur shawl, platform sneakers, and a simple protective face mask that was also white. Lopez paired the look with one of her rotating croc Herm√®s Birkin purses, oversize sunglasses, a slick ponytail, and a matching manicure.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Lopez's recent all-white number wasn't the only time she was seen sporting the monochromatic trend this week. A day prior, at another rehearsal, the superstar wore an emerald-green sweat suit courtesy of Mayfair, which she paired with a tan shearling vest, a coordinating Birkin bag, a star-printed face mask, and chunky sneakers.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Lopez revealed on Instagram yesterday that she will be performing her latest single, "In the Morning," during her sure-to-be showstopping performance on New Year's Eve night.

    "BIG ANNOUNCEMENT: I'm performing #InTheMorning for the very first time on @RockinEve!!!!! It all starts with rehearsals," wrote Lopez alongside a clip of the superstar preparing for the big night with her band and crew.
