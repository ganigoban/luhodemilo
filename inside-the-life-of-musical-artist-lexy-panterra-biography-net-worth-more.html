    Who is Lexy Panterra?

    Being the only daughter of his mother, Elizabeth and father Tony Panterra, a Motocross racer, three children, Lexy Panterra was born on May 22, 1989, in Sonoma County, California in the United States of America. Growing up, she lived moving in between her father’s house in Los Angeles to her mother’s house in Sacramento. 

    By age 8, she started racing motorcycles. Her first race was at Los Angeles County Raceway. After winning multiple races, she retired at the age of 11. She decided to stop after her father seriously injured himself in a Motocross accident. 

    She later got involved with dancing, and singing, at a young age. She was trained in ballet and hip-hop. She eventually learned how to twerk from her step-cousins at the age of 12. During her high school years, she got involved in theater productions, performing in multiple school musicals.

    What is Lexy Panterra’s profession?

    Regarding her career as a dancer, she was trained in ballet and hip-hop. She learned how to twerk from her step-cousins at the age of 12. During her high school years, she got involved in theater productions, performing in multiple school musicals. 

    In 2013, she gained inspiration to incorporate twerking into her dance/exercise routines. This was right after Miley Cyrus’s twerking performance at the 2013 MTV Video Music Awards ceremony. She founded her twerking exercise program LexTwerkOut in January 2014. She created a concept that combined twerking with fitness. She gained recognition for that and twerked at several events in major cities, including New York City, Hollywood, and Las Vegas.

    With her twerking exercise program LexTwerkOut, she has her students warm up by stretching and doing some cardio exercises. To promote her studio and classes, she created a YouTube channel under the same name. The channel gained a substantial following at an exponential rate. She has over 2.1 million subscribers on the channel.
