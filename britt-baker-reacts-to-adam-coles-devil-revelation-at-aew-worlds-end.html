    Britt Baker has been linked with Adam Cole for several years as they are in a long-term, committed relationship. Therefore, when The Devil story started in AEW, many fans believed Baker was The Devil. At AEW Worlds End, it was revealed that Adam Cole was the Devil that had been harassing MJF for so long and now Britt Baker has reacted to this shocking reveal.

    Ringside News provided comprehensive coverage of AEW Worlds End tonight. It was truly a night of memorable moments and eye-popping action, but the company saved the best for last.

    MJF defended the AEW World Championship against Samoa Joe in the main event of AEW Worlds End. MJF was banged up ahead of the match and he simply wasn’t able to thwart Joe’s offense. In the end, The Samoan Submission Machine defeated MJF to become the new AEW World Champion.

    Right after that, it was revealed that Adam Cole was the Devil, alongside his henchmen The Kingdom, Roderick Strong, and Wardlow. Britt Baker took to Twitter immediately after this revelation and simply reminded fans that she was not The Devil, as Baker was just The Bully.

    ‘‘I told you guys I’m not the devil.

    I’m the bully…. remember?”

    Britt Baker is currently out of action due to injury, but that doesn’t mean she won’t be by Adam Cole’s side once she returns. Only time will tell how AEW will book The Devil’s storyline, as fans hope 2024 turns out to be a great year for the company.

    What do you think of what Britt Baker had to say? Are you surprised that Adam Cole is The Devil? Let us know in the comments section below!
