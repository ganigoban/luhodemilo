    In our North Necluda Sky Crystal Zelda Tears of the Kingdom Josiu Shrine Quest guide, we are going to show you how to complete this mission, step by step. It’s a little tricky, but nothing too bad. That’s what we’ll be focusing on. While you’re in the zone, be sure to explore it in detail for goodies, such as the item dispenser and the chest hanging off the bridge you’ll be rotating.

    How to Complete North Necluda Sky Crystal Shrine Quest in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom

    To complete the North Necluda Sky Crystal Josiu shrine quest in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom, you need to find the crystal and bring it back to the shrine. If you don’t know where the starting position is, check out the map screenshot below. When you interact with the location of Josiu, you will get a laser of sorts pointing you towards the crystal. Getting there isn’t easy, though. First off, follow the light until you find the artifact that lets you rotate the curved bridge. Turn it so that it connects the area on your left and across from you, as shown below. Turn around and go the opposite direction and find a place on your right that will let you jump over onto the adjoining area, as shown below.

    The next step to complete the Zelda Tears of the Kingdom North Necluda Sky Crystal Josiu shrine quest is to head up the stairs. At the top, you’ll find one of those “cannons” that can launch you into the air. Aim it towards the platforms that you’ve connected and fire. use the glider to land safely and just walk to the green, egg-shaped crystal. Pick it up and carry it to the bridge to “safety.” Drop your cargo and make your way back to the rotating bridge. Turn it so that it now connects the platform where you left the crystal and the platform the shrine is on. All that’s left is to pick the crystal back up and take it to where the shrine should be.
