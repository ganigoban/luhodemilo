    Blake Lively, Ryan Reynolds Spotted Strolling in New York City

    Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds were spotted bundled up sans kids in New York City yesterday. It was a rare sighting from the ultra-private couple, especially considering Lively gave birth to the pairs third child only a few months prior.

    28 Aug 2024, 00:00
    • Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds were spotted bundled up in New York City on Wednesday without their children.
    • The two actors welcomed their third daughter last October.

    Blake Lively and Ryan Reynolds were spotted bundled up sans kids in New York City yesterday.

    It was a rare sighting from the ultra-private couple, especially considering Lively gave birth to the pair’s third child only a few months prior.

    Lively donned an oversized army-green parka with a matching knit beanie, black skinny jeans, and leather lace-up Vans sneakers. She accessorized the look with a multicolored, striped scarf and left her signature, sun-streaked waves down. Reynolds looked casually dapper in a blue Canali hooded field jacket, black straight-leg pants, thick-rimmed glasses, and brown suede oxfords.

    The couple welcomed their third child—a daughter—in October but have not revealed her specific birthdate or her name. The pair are also proud parents of two other girls, James, 4, and Inez, 3.

    Matt Winkelmeyer//Getty Images

    Though the couple are staunchly protective when it comes to their children’s privacy, Reynolds did share that his oldest daughter is already showing signs of catching the acting bug.

    “She wants to be an actor. Yep. I’m fine if she wants to be an actor. Go ahead. You know, in school, do after school stuff like that,” said Reynolds on The Tonight Show Starring Jimmy Fallon. “Right now, it’s every day is like a recital. She’s singing songs, she’s doing little plays at home, all that stuff, but being a child actor in movies and stuff, that’s a whole other ball of wax.”
