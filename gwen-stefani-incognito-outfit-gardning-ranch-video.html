    Gwen Stefani may be the ultimate pop-star with her signature blonde locks, quirky outfits and statement talons, but she's also not afraid to get her hands dirty at her husband Blake Shelton's ranch.

    The Voice star took to Instagram Stories on Thursday to share clips of her day in Oklahoma on the 1,300 acre ranch, where she was wearing a straw sunhat, aqua blue sunglasses and a red bandana wrapped around her face.

    The singer was then seen planting seeds by hand, digging her long orange nails into the soil in a flowerbed on the site.

    The No Doubt singer and Blake typically spend the majority of their time together in Los Angeles at their $14 million house in Encino, but lately they have been spending lots of time in the country on the ranch.

    Last month, the mother-of-three took to TikTok to share a sweet video of herself alongside her husband, touring around the ranch in a 4x4. The Hollaback Girl singer looked casual in a black T-shirt, camo print trousers and a cap, but in true popstar style, she accessorised with layered necklaces. Blake donned a camo shirt and a stone-coloured cap for the outing.
