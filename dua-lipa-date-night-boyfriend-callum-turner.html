    Dua Lipa has seemingly publicly confirmed her rumored romance with British actor Callum Turner, heading out on a date with him on Sunday night.

    The new couple were spotted enjoying an evening out in Santa Monica following Turner’s participation in a Q&A at the Aero Theatre, Page Six reported. For the low-key outing, Lipa and Turner wore contrasting outfits that showed off each of their own personal senses of style.

    Lipa opted for an elegant long black coat, which she layered over a white collared button-down and creamy straight-leg pants. She finished off the ensemble with metallic open-toe sandals, a matching metallic clutch, and a sculptural pair of silver pendant earrings with a ruby-red gemstone that matched her cherry-red hair.

    As for Turner, he looked cozy in a military-green knit turtleneck sweater layered over a white T-shirt, plus khaki cargo pants and white sneakers.

    BACKGRID

    Rumors that Lipa and Turner were dating emerged last week, after TMZ published a video of the two slow dancing and seemingly kissing at the after-party for the Los Angeles premiere of Masters of the Air. Lipa presumably attended the event to support Turner, who stars in the Apple TV+ drama series alongside Austin Butler, Sawyer Spielberg, Barry Keoghan, and Lauren McQueen.

    The actor’s last known relationship was with The Crown star Vanessa Kirby, whom he dated for four years before the two called it quits in February 2020. Meanwhile, Lipa was most recently linked to French director Romain Gavras.
