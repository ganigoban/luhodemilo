    Damian Priest is unquestionably one of WWE’s most talented WWE performers. He oozes charisma and is capable of cutting spectacular promos with his deep voice. It appears that now Damian is planning to make everyone’s heart race by getting a new tattoo.

    October is known as “Spooky Month,” and it causes many to embrace their dark side. Damian Priest is a huge fan of heavy metal and rock music. He also has the in-ring appearance of a rock star.

    Metalheads seldom pass up an opportunity to show their dark sides, and Damian Priest did the same to celebrate Halloween. The Judgement Day member got new tattoos on his right arm. The tattoo depicts a skull with the letters ‘CDMX’ written on its head.

    Damian’s tattoo was done by a man in the picture while a woman gave him the gothic look. It’s unclear at this time who his artist was. Damian shared it on Instagram, and his followers were thrilled. A fan wrote, “Love this! What an energy!!”

    Damian is certainly one of the best all-around WWE Superstars. Let’s see what Damian has in store for us next. To get the latest updates, keep an eye on Ringside News.
