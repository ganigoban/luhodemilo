Where Are They Now Little Rascals?

08 Sep 2024, 00:00

The “Little Rascals,” also known as “Our Gang,” was a series of American comedy short films about a group of poor neighborhood children and their adventures. Created by Hal Roach, the series was produced from 1922 to 1944 and is noted for showing children behaving in a relatively natural way. The “Little Rascals” has become a cherished part of American entertainment history, and many of the child actors who starred in the series went on to lead various lives after their time in the spotlight.
