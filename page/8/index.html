What is the difference between deck mounted and curb mount skylights?

09 Sep 2024, 00:00

Answer What’s the difference between a “deck-mounted” skylight and a “curb-mounted” skylight? On top of a framed aperture in the roof, curb-mounted skylights are installed. Deck-mounted skylights attach to the roof sheathing directly, eliminating the need for a framed curb.   As a result, what does a deck mounted skylight imply? Skylights installed on the deck The roof deck is immediately coupled to a deck-mounted skylight. It has a built-in frame/curb that you must seal with waterproof materials (underlayments).
