Answer To clean your seashells, use a solution of bleach. Fill a saucepan halfway with water and bleach and set aside. Soak the shells in the solution for a few minutes. You may then take the shells from the solution after the coating has been removed. Allow your shells to dry fully after properly rinsing them. Baby oil or mineral oil may be used to restore the sheen to the shells.
