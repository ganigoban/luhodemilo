    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Kylie Jenner stepped out in a sultry look Tuesday night to attend a fashion party in New York City; and with her, she brought her mini-me, daughter Stormi Webster.

    The two attended the launch of Jean Paul Gaultier's ready-to-wear Flowers collection, held at The Webster. For the occasion, the makeup mogul wore a totally sheer spring dress from the collection—and her daughter wore a miniature version of the piece.

    Jenner's look was a completely see-through beige dress with a large print featuring red, orange, and yellow roses. Underneath, she wore only a nude bra, a silky nude corset, and a matching thong—all which were exposed due to the dress' sheer fabric.

    The mom of two paired the dress with silky, pointy blush heels, a red clutch, a diamond ear cuff, a silver ring, and shimmering rosy makeup. Her hair was styled in an elegant updo.

    MEGA//Getty Images

    Jenner was pictured stepping out of The Ritz-Carlton hotel to head to the event, hand-in-hand with Stormi. The five-year-old looked adorable in a colorful long-sleeve dress with an abstract painterly and floral print. The piece was also sheer, but included a matte minidress underneath. Stormi wore the dress with white sneakers and tiny diamond hoop earrings. Her hair was pulled up into two braided pigtails, and she had on a cute pink-and-green manicure. Per photos, she couldn't stop smiling while walking with her famous mom.

    The sweet mom-and-daughter outing came one day after Jenner joined sisters Kendall Jenner and Kim Kardashian at the 2023 Met Gala. To attend fashion's biggest night, she wore a fiery red gown with a train that doubled as a shiny cornflower blue cape, custom-made for her by Jean Paul Gaultier.
