    Kylie Jenner just debuted a new, jaw-dropping bikini look.

    This afternoon, the Kylie Cosmetics founder shared yet another set of swimsuit pics, in which she poses while sitting by the pool in a luxe cushioned wood chair. The star's tiny olive-green two-piece by Sommer Swim is comprised of tiny string bottoms and a tie-front bandeau top, featuring ruched detailing, long trailing strings, and a circle peekaboo feature at the center. She elevated the suit with an array of gold accessories, including a dainty belly chain, a stack of bangles, and layered pendant necklaces.

    For the casual day, the beauty mogul kept her makeup minimal with a nude lip and dark lip liner, while wearing her hair in loose waves.

    She captioned the pics, "happy saturday."
