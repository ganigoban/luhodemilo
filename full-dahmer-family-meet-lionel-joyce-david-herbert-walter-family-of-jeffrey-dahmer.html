    Since October 2022, Netflix users have been stunned by the cannibalistic monstrosity of Jeffrey Dahmer and many want to know his family members and where they are.

    The sheer audacity of the crimes of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer in Monster: The Jeffrey Dahmer Story and the docuseries Conversations With a Killer: The Jeffrey Dahmer Tapes shows just how evil Jeffrey Dahmer is and MyNewsGh.com is covering all the angles for our readers.

    In both shows, viewers learn about the 17 murders Jeffrey Dahmer committed between the years 1978 and 1991.

    Jeffrey Dahmer Family Members

    Jeffrey Dahmer’s father Lionel Dahmer, his mother Joyce Dahmer, stepmother Shari and others have been featured in the series.

    Jeffrey Dahmer was born May 21, 1960 in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, United States. He died on November 28, 1994 at the Columbia Correctional Institution, Portage, Wisconsin, United States

    His Parents are Lionel Dahmer and Joyce Dahmer

    His known Sibling is David Dahmer.

    His Grandparents were Herbert Walter Dahmer, Catherine Jemima Hughes Dahmer and Floyd “Rocky” Flint
